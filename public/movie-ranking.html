The Best Movies According To Solomiia

My top 3 movies of all-time

Oppenheimer

The story centers on the American physicist Julius Robert Oppenheimer, a professor of physics at the University of California, Berkeley and a member of the US National Academy of Sciences, who led the development of the atomic bomb during World War II.

Gran Turismo

Since childhood, the protagonist has been obsessed with car racing, and he practices his driving skills on PlayStation game simulators. One day, the young gamer manages to become a member of the innovative GT Academy program organized by Nissan and Sony. This unique competition combines the real and virtual worlds and gives you the opportunity to feel like you are driving a real car.

Three Steps Above Heaven

Three Meters Above the Sky tells the story of how human destinies can be unpredictable and how love is a mysterious feeling that is difficult to control. The main characters of this story, Babi and Ace, are very different in character and social status. Babi is a sweet and innocent girl from a wealthy family, while Ace is an irresponsible, impulsive guy who likes to take risks and engage in dangerous activities.
